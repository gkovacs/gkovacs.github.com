Facebook Mass-Friend Inviter (for Event Spamming)

FB Mass-Friend Inviter. Press Ctrl-Shift-B to show bookmarks bar (Command-Shift-B on Macs). Then drag this link to the bookmarks bar. Then open the Invite Friends dialog for your event on Facebook and click this bookmark in the bookmarks bar. It will check all your friends. You may need to scroll through your friends list and click the bookmark again. Then send the invites.
